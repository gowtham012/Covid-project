COVID-19 detection using CT Scan images

Objective of Project

Based on Lung Ct Scan images our app should identify whether a person got infected with CORONAVIRUS, based on which medication should be recommended. Such an app could be helpful in detecting CORONAVIRUS faster and hence faster treatment can be provided to the user.

• Medical officials can monitor the society to make health policies.
